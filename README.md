# Dandelion Drift 🌬️🌼

A minimalist, physics-based "Zen" game where you guide a delicate dandelion seed on a journey across a shifting landscape.

## The Journey

In "Dandelion Drift," you play as a lone dandelion fluff carried by the wind. Your goal is to stay aloft, navigating through the air while avoiding the hazards of the natural world. It's a game of rhythm, timing, and tranquility.

### Gameplay Mechanics

*   **Stay Aloft:** Tap or press a key to create a "puff" of air that lifts your seed.
*   **Avoid Hazards:** Navigate away from birds and ensure you don't fall into the water below.
*   **Sunlight Power-up:** Collect rare sunlight droplets to gain **Lightness** (reduced gravity) and **Immunity** (invincibility against birds and water) for a short duration.
*   **The Grace Period:** Every journey begins with a 5-meter window of calm to help you find your rhythm.

## Controls

The game is designed for both desktop and mobile play:

*   **Desktop:**
    *   `Spacebar`, `Up Arrow`, or `W` to puff air.
    *   Mouse Click also works!
*   **Mobile/Tablet:**
    *   Tap anywhere on the screen to puff air.

## Visuals & Aesthetic

*   **Minimalist Zen:** A soft, morning-sky color palette designed to be easy on the eyes.
*   **Dynamic Background:** Parallax rolling hills that create a sense of peaceful movement.
*   **Animated Entities:** Birds with flapping wings and a gently waving water surface.
*   **Ultra-Fluffy Seed:** A procedurally drawn dandelion head with 64 delicate filaments.

## How to Play

1.  Download the `index.html` file.
2.  Open it in any modern web browser.
3.  Click **Start Journey** and see how far the wind can carry you!
